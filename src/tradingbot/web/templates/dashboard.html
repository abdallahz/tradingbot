📖 Trading Terms Legend
▾
Key Levels
VWAP Volume-Weighted Average Price — the average price weighted by volume throughout the day. Institutional benchmark; price above VWAP = bullish bias.
EMA 9 / EMA 20 Exponential Moving Averages (9-period fast, 20-period slow). When price holds above both = uptrend. EMA9 crossing below EMA20 = bearish.
Key Support Strongest floor below price (highest of VWAP, EMA20, pre-market low, previous close). Used for stop-loss placement.
Key Resistance Nearest ceiling above price (pre-market high or bar resistance). Used as the first profit target (TP1).
ATR Average True Range — measures volatility. Used as a buffer below support for stop placement so normal noise doesn't trigger the stop.
Chart Patterns
🚩 Bull Flag Strong up-move (pole) followed by a controlled pullback with lower highs (flag). Bullish continuation pattern.
⚡ Breakout Price crossing above resistance with volume confirmation (≥1.5× average). Signals a new leg up.
↗️ Support Bounce Price touches a support level and bounces up with increasing volume — buyers defending the level.
🔨 Hammer Bullish reversal candle: small body at the top, long lower wick (≥2× body). Shows buyers rejected lower prices.
🟢 Bull Engulfing Green candle fully engulfs the previous red candle. Strong bullish reversal signal.
🔴 Bear Engulfing Red candle fully engulfs the previous green candle. Strong bearish reversal signal.
〰️ Doji Open ≈ Close (tiny body, long wicks). Signals indecision — potential reversal ahead.
📈 Above VWAP Price trading above VWAP indicates institutional buying pressure and bullish intraday bias.
Signals & Metrics
Gap % Percentage difference between today's open and yesterday's close. Large gaps signal strong overnight catalysts.
RelVol Relative Volume — today's volume vs. the average. RelVol 2.0× = twice normal activity, confirming real interest.
Volume Spike Recent volume exceeds the per-minute average by a multiplier. Confirms that the move has conviction, not just drift.
R:R (Risk/Reward) Ratio of potential profit (TP1) to potential loss (stop). Minimum 2:1 required — you risk $1 to make $2+.
Confluence Score 0–100 score from combining all detected patterns. Higher = more signals agreeing on the same direction.
Catalyst Score 0–100 score from overnight news research (SEC filings, earnings, press releases, RSS feeds). ≥40 = noteworthy catalyst.
TP1 / TP2 Take-Profit targets. TP1 = key resistance level. TP2 = TP1 + 1R extension (extra reward if momentum continues).
